GUESTS

Pierre Hermé

Pierre Hermé is the ‘Picasso’ of pastries. He is notably most famous for his flavoured macarons that are rich and unusual in taste. His baking skills have made him well known and he has written over a dozen books. He is the youngest person to ever be named “France’s Pastry Chef of the Year”. Currently, he manages two Michelin star awarded restaurants in France.

Gale Gand

Award winning Gale Gand is a Chicago-based pastry chef. She has hosted TV shows, won awards and written books on desserts. She began her career as a line cook in 1976 and then became a pastry chef in New York. She co-opened restaurants and was the host of Sweet Dreams cooking show from 2000-2008.
